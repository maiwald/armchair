Armchair: The RPG Workshop

Feel free to play around with it: https://my-armchair.com/alpha.

Armchair is currently in alpha.

Armchair is a program for writing branching interactive dialogue. Typically found in (c)RPGs but you can use it for other types of computer games, too.

It only uses cookies or local storage to save your state of the world. No data is ever sent anywhere. I try to keep save-files compatible during the alpha; no guarantees, though.
